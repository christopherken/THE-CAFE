About

THE CAFE was founded by Brian Scott in 2004 at Queens,NY. The opening ceremony happened on the 13th of March by 2:00pm.It started as a place for snacks and coffee with about 14 staffs , it was later expanded to a place for a wide variety of snacks and lots of different drinks. The staff was also increased to about 40 staffs. After about 2 years new branches were opened in 3 different places and states.

Presently, THE CAFE now operates in over 15 states with over 47 branches and over 6,000 staffs and a serving capacity of about 150 people per service.
